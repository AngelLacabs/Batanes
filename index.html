Batanes: The Green Paradise

Known for its rolling hills, stone houses, and breathtaking seascapes, Batanes offers a peaceful escape and untouched natural beauty.

Tukon Church

Also known as the Mt. Carmel Chapel, Tukon Church sits atop a hill overlooking Basco, offering a breathtaking view of Batanes' rolling landscapes and sea.

Basco Lighthouse

Standing proudly on Naidi Hills, the Basco Lighthouse offers a perfect view of the West Philippine Sea — a symbol of guidance and peace in Batanes.

The Enchanting Islands of Batanes

Batanes, the northernmost province of the Philippines, is a haven of rolling hills, stone houses, and stunning coastlines. Its untouched beauty and serene atmosphere make it a perfect escape for travelers seeking peace and adventure.
